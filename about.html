Atlanta based creative designer and Photographer with extensive international and cross-cultural experiences.

Beatrice draws on decade of digital experience. Her approch to the work she creates is minimalistic but also with elegance. She has always been a story-teller, before stumbled upon web developement and photography. It all started with creating concept and metrics to tracks potential users on my social platform and being her own photographer, until the day she decided to become professional business branding.

The concept DameDucharme Design was created to help businesses worldwide reach targeted audiences and promote their products and/or services to potential customers in a much more efficient, cost-effective and hassle-free way. We design and develop services for customers specializing creating stylish, modern websites, web services and online stores.

Prior to moving to Atlanta, Beatrice worked at Dunkrirk, Lyons and in Paris as a designer and an software engineer.
